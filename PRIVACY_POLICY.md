# Privacy Policy for ConjuMate

**Last Updated: April 2, 2026**

ConjuMate ("we," "our," or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is handled.

## 1. Data We Collect
*   **Website Content:** When you perform a lookup, the extension sends the text you have selected and its immediate surrounding sentence (context) to our servers to provide accurate conjugations and translations.
*   **Anonymous Identifier:** We generate a random, non-identifiable UUID stored in your browser's local storage. This is used solely to manage daily usage limits (rate limiting) for our free translation and conjugation services.
*   **IP Address:** Standard server logs (via our Cloudflare Workers host) temporarily process your IP address to complete the API request.

## 2. How Data is Used
*   To provide instant verb conjugations and translations.
*   To enforce fair-usage limits (75 lookups and 100 translations per day).
*   We **do not** sell, trade, or rent your data to third parties.

## 3. Data Processing
Translations are processed through reputable third-party providers (DeepL or Google Translate). No personally identifiable information is sent to these providers; only the text selection you requested.

## 4. Your Rights
You can reset your usage history and anonymous ID at any time by clearing the extension's storage or uninstalling the extension.
